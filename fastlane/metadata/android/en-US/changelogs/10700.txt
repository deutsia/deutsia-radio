## What's New
**HLS Stream Support**
Stations using HLS (.m3u8) now play and record correctly. This resolves playback issues with stations from the RadioBrowser API.
**fMP4/CMAF Support**
Added fMP4/CMAF playback and recording for custom station links.
**Tor Auto-Detection**
The app now auto-detects a Tor SOCKS5 proxy (port 9050) from InviZible, Orbot, etc. and routes traffic through it automatically.
## Security
Fixed cover art leaking to the lockscreen when disabled in settings.
